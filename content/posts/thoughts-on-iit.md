I used to hate the idea of engineering. Just like the inherent left-leaning attitude in youth, I abhored the idea of pursuing something that everyone was doing. In fact, I was almost going to do a BS (Research) at IISc. Almost ..., until 12th September.
{{< figure src="/images/iisc_admission_withdrawal.png" caption="IISc Admission Withdrawal" >}}

The thing is, on 11th September, IIT Bombay decided to release the results for JEE Advanced 2022. I was ranked 264 in the country (more on that in a bit) and 24 among the Economically Weaker Section ([EWS reservation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economically_Weaker_Section)) aspirants.
